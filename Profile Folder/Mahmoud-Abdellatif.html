Mahmoud Abdellatif

Chief Operating Officer at International Socioeconomics Laboratory

Mahmoud Abdellatif is the Chief Operating Officer of the International Socioeconomics Laboratory. He is interested in building machine learning software, computer science, law, and business. Receiving International Attention on his work and commended by Oxford he has conducted research on Artificial Intelligence and Machine learning and has been published in multiple international journals.

Ranking first in several math olympiads he has been known for his love of math. He has won several competitions from his robot that helps depressed students and has received honors and commendations from many universities and gained commendation from the Trump Administration and now the Biden Administration for his knowledge of Software Development.
