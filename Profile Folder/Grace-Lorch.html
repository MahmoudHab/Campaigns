Grace Lorch

Chief Finacial Officer at International Socioeconomics Laboratory

Grace Lorch has been with Finxerunt since July 2020, when she joined as an intern in the FINX Summer Student Leadership Marketing and Finance Program. Her interest in finance lead her to the program, where she was inspired by her talented advisors. She decided to continue with Finxerunt after completing her internship, wanting to help Finxerunt expand its online presence. Grace now serves as a director of the Digital Marketing Committee and the CFO for the organization. She loves her role in Finxerunt and is glad to be working with such a passionate group of people.
