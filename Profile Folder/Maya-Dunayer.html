Maya Dunayer

Editor in Chief

Maya Dunayer is a senior at Stuyvesant High School with a passion for law, politics, and economics. She joined the International Socioeconomics Laboratory in February of 2021 and has become an Editor in Chief, helping to put together the Laboratory’s journal Across the Spectrum of Socioeconomics.

Aside from ISL, Maya is heavily involved in various social science disciplines, working with various organizations that specialize in law and economics. She also writes for the Stuyvesant Spectator’s Opinions Department, runs the Stuyvesant Save the Children Club, and is a part of StuyMUN. Maya's love of debating has allowed her and inspired her to co-found a company specializing in debate tutoring.

In her free time, Maya loves to read, spend time with friends in various parts of the city, and vacation with family.
